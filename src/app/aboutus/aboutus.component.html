About us

Ancient process, Modern Application.

At Fusion Food, we're playing with one such age-old food processing technique: air-drying. For centuries together, mankind has been harnessing the power of the sun to preserve foodstuff by drying so that it lasts longer and taste better. Our Instant Food is healthy & also good for the environment. Keep scrolling to see who Fusion Food is for, how it is made and what makes it a healthy snack.

- Why Fusion Food is Air Dried
Air-Dried
  • Low carbon footprint- more efficient process.
  • Smaller batch - Low MOQs.
  • Slow process helps develop unique flavours.
  • No added sugars or chemicals
  • Cheapest of the lot.
Candied
  • 50% Sugar.
  • Chemical preservatives added
  • More of a sugar candy taste profile than the fruit itself.
  • Difficult to infuse.
Freeze Dried
  • High carbon footprint-longer energy intensive process.
  • Larger batch- higher MOQs
  • No custom options available
  • Most expensive of the lot.

How is Fusion Food different?

Mode to Order

Our process is small batch and slow and hence we can cater to custom air-drying requirements of all consumers.

1X The Weight,
10X The Flavor

Each gram of HUKI is equivalent to flavors of 10g of the fruit or veg. The moisture is removed, but the flavors get concentrated!

Made In India

Right from our ingredients to our drying & packaging machinery, everything is proudly made in India.

Versatile

Fusion Food can be ground to make flavourful and 100% natural powders. They can be had as is or even used as an infusion.
